**Administrator (you):**  
- Logs into the back office  
- Adds/edits/deletes boat listings with:  
    - Images  
    - Description  
    - Specifications  
    - Category  
    - Price  

**Visitor:**  
- Homepage  
- Lists boats by category  
- Searches for boats by specifications, price, etc.  
- Views a specific boat  
    - Clicks on "I'm interested in this boat"  
        - Enters name, surname, email, and comment  
        - The system sends you an email  
- Clicks on "Sell my boat"  
    - Enters name, surname, email, comment, and photos  
    - The system sends you an email
